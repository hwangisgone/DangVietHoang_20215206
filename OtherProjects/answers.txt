- What happens if users choose "Cancel"?
	Answer:	If the users chose "Cancel", the dialog defaults to "No".

- How to customize the options to users, e.g. only two options: "Yes" and "No", OR "I do" and "I dont'"?
	Answer: 
		Only two "Yes" and "No" option can be achieved with JOptionPane.YES_NO_OPTION as the 4th argument of JOptionPane.showConfirmDialog 

		Button Customization ("I do" & "I don't") can be done with the seventh argument of JOptionPane.showOptionDialog

		Demonstration is in the code of ChoosingOption.java
